How To Play

Tic-Tac-Toe with a Twist: Minefield Rules

1. Mine Placement : A single mine is randomly placed in one of the 9 boxes, but it remains invisible to the players.

2. Mine Avoidance : Each player must avoid stepping on the mine for first 3 moves. If a player successfully avoids the mine, their turn continues.

3. Game Over : If a player lands on the mine, they instantly lose the game, regardless of their chances of winning.

4. Luck of the Draw : This game is heavily influenced by luck, as the mine's location is random and hidden.

5. Unpredictable Outcomes : It's possible to win a game that seemed lost or lose a game that was almost won, all due to the mine's presence.

6. Winning Conditions : To emerge victorious, a player must win the game by normal Tic-Tac-Toe rules while successfully avoiding the mine for first 3 moves.


Remember, in this high-stakes game, luck plays a significant role, and the outcome is never certain until the final move. Will you dare to take the risk and play?
